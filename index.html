Discover Gaynor Minden

Ballet

Ballet is a form of dance that combines beauty, grace, and technical skill to create a unique art form. It is a highly structured and codified art form. I am passionate about ballet because it is a beautiful and timeless art form, which is constantly evolving, and it is a great way to express myself and explore my creativity. Ballet has shaped me into the person that I am today, since I started at age 6 and I will never stop loving it.

Contemporary Art

I love contemporary art and all its form of expression to address current social and political issues such as identity, gender, race, culture and globalization. I am interested in paintings, drawings, sculptures, performances and digital art that incorporates contemporary art.

Parametric Architecture

I am passionate about parametric architecture because it offers a way to create complex, intricate and highly detailed designs. It allows thundering creative freedom and flexibility throughout the design process of striking and unique products.

Art Nouveau

I am also passionate about Art Nouveau because that style combines both the classical and modern elements of art. Art Nouveau is characterized by highly-stylized, organic forms and highly decorative motifs, often inspired by nature with organic shapes.
